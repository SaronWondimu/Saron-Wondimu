About Us

Zoe Psychological and Training Service is dedicated to providing expert mental health support, emotional well-being training, and counseling for adolescents, youth, and communities. We specialize in one-on-one therapy, trauma healing, gender-based violence intervention, and mental health awareness campaigns.

Our approach is rooted in compassion, evidence-based therapy, and community-driven impact. We believe in creating safe spaces for healing, growth, and self-discovery.
